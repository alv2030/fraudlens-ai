# Business Impact

FraudLens AI demonstrates how a financial institution can reduce fraud losses and improve investigator efficiency by combining machine learning, rule-based scoring, real-time streaming, and explainable AI.

Potential business outcomes:

- Faster detection of high-risk transactions
- Lower fraud losses through earlier alerting
- More consistent triage using standardized risk scores
- Better investigator decisions through reason codes and explanations
- Improved model governance with versioning and evaluation reports
